Composer in residence at BØRSEN Kulturhus, Tangen, Norway 2024

Musikhuset VÆKST residency with Damkapellet at Musikhuset København run by Danish Composers Society 2023-2024

'Chants', commissioned by Koncertkirken at Winter Jazz Festival 2023

'A Sounding Selfie' Multichannel installation. Composer in residency at Andersabo, Unnaryd, Sweden together with Damkapellet members Irene Bianco and Josefine Weber Hansen 2022.
